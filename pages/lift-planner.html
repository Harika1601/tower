Lift calculations help engineers determine whether a crane can safely lift a specific load at a given radius.

Important Calculation Factors
  • Load weight and dimensions
  • Lifting radius from crane center
  • Boom length and angle
  • Crane load chart capacity
  • Environmental conditions
